üìä What's Included in Meta Tags

Open Graph Tags

  • og:title - Real article title
  • og:description - Article excerpt or content preview
  • og:image - Original article image
  • og:type - "article"
  • og:url - Article URL

Article-Specific Tags

  • article:author - Article author
  • article:section - Article category
  • article:published_time - Publish date
  • article:tag - Article tags

Twitter Card Tags

  • twitter:card - "summary_large_image"
  • twitter:title - Article title
  • twitter:description - Article description
  • twitter:image - Article image
